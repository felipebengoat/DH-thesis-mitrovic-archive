Sehr geehrtes Mitglied / Dear Member,
We confirm receipt of your letter of 11 December and have the pleasure of enclosing herewith the annual stamp for the year 1935, which you will wish to affix to the front of the membership card issued to you at the time.
We are also enclosing a copy of our latest balance sheet as of 31 October of last year for your information, and regret that we were not in a position today to issue an annual journal as in the previous year.
Your annual membership fee is covered until 31 October of this year, however at the rate that was valid for the previous year. The fee has however been set by the General Assembly for the year 1934/35 at $84.- for ordinary members, so that we will endeavour to collect the remaining difference of $12.- in our favour, as agreed, from your parents.
With best wishes for your wellbeing we remain with
Bergheil
Deutscher Ausflugverein, Santiago
Sektion Chile des Deutschen und Oesterr. Alpenvereins

Schriftführer. Praes.
Separately we are sending you a copy of the Sunday edition with 2 enclosures. Report on the most important matters of the past club year.
