Lapad, 25.II.1940
Dear Luchito, yesterday I forgot to ask you in my letter. Would you be so kind as to invest the money 000.30 that I sent you for Ped in the best interest-bearing way for the time being, in my name, however in such a way that you or he (Ped.) can dispose of it at any time without further ado.
Many thanks and warm greetings,
Your Aunt
Elisa
