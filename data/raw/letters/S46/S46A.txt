In accordance with your request, we are enclosing herewith a letter of introduction to the Viennese sections of the German and Austrian Alpine Club, together with a list of addresses of all sections based in Vienna and the surrounding area. We are further enclosing your membership card for the year 1934, with the note that this card must be renewed for 1935, for which however we are only in a position to do so once we have received the corresponding annual stamps for the following year from the administrative committee.
For the purpose of forwarding the membership card for 1935, we therefore ask you to provide us with your Viennese address.
Mit Bergheil!
