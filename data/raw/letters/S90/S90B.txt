To the Rectorate
of the Hochschule für Baukunst,
Weimar.
I am of Chilean nationality. In Chile I obtained my Abitur. At the Academy of Fine Arts I studied architecture for two semesters.
I had the intention of continuing my studies here in Germany and also completing my final examination as an architect. Having heard of the first National Socialist Hochschule für Baukunst in Weimar, I had a prospectus sent to me. According to this prospectus the school seemed very suitable for my further studies. In Weimar I was very disappointed, because most of the points mentioned in the prospectus did not correspond to the facts.
What mattered to me above all was to get to know the National Socialist architectural philosophy in the new Germany, and I was unfortunately very disappointed in this regard in the lectures of Prof. Schultze-Naumburg.
It is regrettable that this school, which is beginning to seek recognition abroad through exaggerated statements in prospectuses and through many newspaper advertisements, has disappointed me so. For this reason I have submitted my withdrawal.
L. Mitrovich-Balbontin
Weimar, 22 June 1936
Am Horn 39.
