Miguel Davila Carson
Arquitecto
Catedral 2109 - Teléf. 83953
Santiago
Santiago de Chile, 5 September 1934.
To Messrs. Bauräte
Emil Hoppe and Otto Schönthal,
Ungargasse N°4,
Vienna III.
My most esteemed gentlemen!
I take the liberty of sending you these lines through my friend Mr. Luis Mitrovich, so that you may see that I always preserve a fond memory of the time spent with you.
Rarely do compatriots of mine come to Vienna, and even more rarely one who wishes to study architecture there like Mr. Mitrovich, who wishes to visit the Kunstgewerbeschule in the certain hope of getting something good from it.
It is a deep pleasure for me to know that my warmest greetings will reach you through the hand of a friend, and knowing the kindness of the Messrs. Bauräte, I ask you to guide the student in a foreign land, as you did with me years ago.
In grateful memory, greetings to you
with respect,
Miguel Davila Carson
