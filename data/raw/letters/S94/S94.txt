Lorna and I are off to Palm Beach Friday, where I can visit our project under construction. I shall be taking my sketch pad as I have a number of drawings to do for our project on the Boston Waterfront / Seaport Area.
I am planning to go back to Vail in March to ski with clients - and Zermatt (1st time to ski in Europe) in March with the kids. Lorna and I are due to go to Italy - this time to stay near Lucca where we can get to the Coast in an hour and Florence in less - in early June.
Have read of your election of a Socialist leader. I trust it will be very different this time round. Get well, keep well, and keep in touch.
Much love from all of us - Howard
