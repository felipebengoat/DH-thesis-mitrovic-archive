Wuppertal-Barmen, Borkumerstrasse 18, 26 Jan. 37
My dear,
It interests me to learn whether my letter from the beginning of December 36 as well as a card around New Year reached you, for I believe Mr. Brown and his people have suddenly also taken an interest in my postal items. You are an old photography veteran and since I am soon thinking of buying a first-class camera, I would be glad if you could perhaps give me a good "tip" in your next letter on this matter. I have obtained Contax, Leica, Rollei etc. brochures to "study." How did you spend the New Year holidays? I hope soon to hear from you and greet you with
Heil Hitler!
Rolf
Sidenote: How do you like it at Prof. Fahrenkamp's atelier? Fine things in the works? I will also soon be thinking about where I will go after Wuppertal, in the long run it is becoming boring for me here!"
