ACADEMY OF FINE ARTS IN VIENNA
Rectorate Ref. 684/1935 — Vienna, June 21, 1935
Subject: Equalization with a domestic student.

To Mr.
Louis Mitrovich-Balbontin
Student of the Master's Course in Architecture,
Vienna.

The Federal Ministry of Education, by order of the Academic Professors' College, through decree of June 13, 1935, Ref. Zl. 19.493-I/6a, has approved your full equalization with domestic students with regard to the calculation of tuition fees.
You are hereby informed of this in accordance with the above-mentioned decree.
Behrens, e.h.
Acting Rector.
