Technical University of Vienna
R.-Zl. 1898-1933/34.
Vienna, 7 December 1934
To Mr. Luis Mitrovich, in Vienna IV.
Your admission as a regular (extraordinary) auditor at the Technical University of Vienna for the Winter Semester 1934/35 can in principle be considered.
The application for new matriculation (admission application) must be submitted together with the required documents at the Rectorate Chancellery at the earliest opportunity, provided the duly documented admission application has not already been sent.
The examination of the documents required for admission to university study, or rather the final decision on your admission application (your registration for continuation of studies) is reserved for this later point in time.
This notice is to be kept carefully and serves, in conjunction with proof of identity (passport, university identification, registration booklet), as the sole pass entitling entry to the university building.
It is expressly pointed out that the enclosed declaration of obligation must be signed and dated by you personally and submitted upon registration for inscription at the Rectorate. Without this duly completed declaration, inscription will be refused.
The Rector:
Technische Hochschule Wien
