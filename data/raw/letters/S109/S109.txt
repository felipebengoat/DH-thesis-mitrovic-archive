E.H. J.C. Paz 1511 Martinez F.C.G.B.M.
Buenos Aires — Argentina

15 Dec. 1970

Dear Lucho, still I am waiting for an answer to my very detailed letter. I can well imagine that the change of government is connected with many complications — but since we are no politicians — the influence will be bearable.
My work has already brought me twice this year to Europe — but I found nothing over there that could cause me to be dissatisfied here. The invasions from over there have also left their mark with us, but when things become somewhat calmer in Europe again and consolidate here with us — then it can get better again. Whether we will still be able to work then and be involved is another question — for slowly I am getting an unfavorable impression of the architectural novelties.
Despite everything we wish you and your dear wife all the best for Christmas and the New Year.
Warmest greetings,
Himmel
