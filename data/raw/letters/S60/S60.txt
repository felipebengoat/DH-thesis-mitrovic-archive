EBERS & CO. EXPORT/IMPORT 
Hamburg 5, 21 September 1935
Langereihe 29, "Handelshof"
To Mr. Luis Mitrovich-Balbontin, Chilean Legation, Vienna.
Dear Mr. Mitrovich!
I refer to my postcard to you of the 13th of this month. In the meantime we made enquiries on your behalf with the competent authority, based on a maximum allowance of M. 800.- per month (in Reichsmark). We received the following reply:
"In response to your letter we respectfully inform you that under the currently applicable regulations, the use of Registered Mark holdings by persons who have their permanent residence abroad and are temporarily staying in Germany for study purposes is possible, to cover their study and living expenses in Germany."
"The amount of the released funds varies between individual approvals, already in view of the differing cost of living of the applicants. We suggest arranging for the Chilean student in question, upon his arrival in Germany, to submit a release application to us using the enclosed form and attaching a proof of enrollment."
It is therefore not possible to obtain any further binding information before your arrival, but I am of the opinion that the risk of having your living allowance approved can well be managed.
I remain with warm regards,
Yours faithfully,
John Grisar
