Dear Lucho:
On the part of The Architects Collaborative and for me, personally, I would like you to know how much we have appreciated your work here during the past seven years (with a two-year break). Your ability to move comfortably and with enthusiasm in the international world, and particularly in South America, is an invaluable asset. In my estimation this is a quality, together with your training and your understanding of architecture, which makes your contribution unique.
I wish you well on whatever endeavors you may undertake. If at any time anyone would be interested in contacting me, you can be sure that I will give them my highest recommendation.
Very sincerely yours,
