WEIMAR.

Dear Sir,
We acknowledge receipt of your letter of yesterday's date, and, as requested, have sent a Banker's draft for £18. to the Salzburger Kredit- und Wechsel-Bank, Salzburg, requesting them to remit the equivalent in Register Marks through the Diskonto-Gesellschaft, Weimar, to you. Kindly return to us, in due course, the enclosed receipt in duplicate.
The above £18. 0. 0., being on account of the payment for November of £23., we add the balance of £5. to your Reserve a/c., which is now £17. 0. 0.
Yours faithfully,
BABURIZZA & COMPANY LTD.,

Secretary.
ENCL:- Receipt in duplicate.
