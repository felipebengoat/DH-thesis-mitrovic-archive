Solothurn, 1.1.36
Dear Smug!
I thank you for your letter, in which I could establish that you are very much plagued by bad luck. The snow has also melted heavily here, though skiing is still going miserably on the higher slopes. I must say that up to today I have still not managed to get to any sport, because an urgent piece of work turned up at the office where I had to help out. Did you celebrate a romantic ski Christmas in the hut in the company of nice little ski bunnies? Regarding the other matter, it is best that you take the things back. Here it is the same problem. I could only depart for the holidays on Saturday night, because I received the jewelry so late. The train had 70 minutes delay. Did you hear about the great railway disaster that occurred near Weimar? Terrible Christmas calamity.
In Basel, City Architect Schuhmacher (swastikas) was summarily dismissed and expelled for espionage and informer service. Among other things he reported Bonatz, who works in Basel, to Berlin for criticism expressed abroad about the Reich.
Tantas felicidades por el año nuevo te desea
tu amigo q.t.m.b.
Rolf Altenburger
