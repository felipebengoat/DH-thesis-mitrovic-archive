Dear Lucho
Sorry to have Renate type this letter, but my handwriting is deteriorated to the point where it is not fair to give you a problem!
We are of course terribly sorry to hear of your great loss. We knew it was coming and are consoled to hear that Angela did not have to suffer terribly much in the end. We can appreciate how difficult it is for you. Only time will help you gain a perspective on it.
In regard to our itinerary we know nothing further at this point than we wrote to you; we will be receiving the details a month before departure, i.e. during the middle of January, and we will let you know the exact times, airline, hotels etc. as soon as we know. At any event it is probably best to stay in the Hotel Sheridan San Cristobal at the beginning of our trip and spend the three extra nights at the end with you, if that is o.k with you. Of course we hope that you will be able to spend time with us during those first days, Feb. 15/16.
If it is not inconvenient for you we would naturally love to spend the extra 3 days with you and stay at your apartment. We are looking forward to the trip with great anticipation. It will be so wonderful to see you again.
Did you ever receive the fax we sent? We sent the letter also by mail, just to be sure. Too bad the mails take so long.
Renate and I hope that you are finding your way into this new and different life of yours and make the best of it. Our good thoughts and love to you.
Tjark
Renate
