LUIS MITROVIĆ B.
ARQUITECTO
Santiago, Oct. 10, 94
Dear Tjark:
I am very sorry I didn't answer your letter Sep. 9, 94 immediately. I was not in the mood to do so as I was having a very tough time with Angela. She was already hopelessly in agony. For two weeks we had her, lucky in agony so she would suffer no pain, unconscious so she was gone Sep. 14, 94. We have been so happily married for so many years, that it will be very difficult to get used to live without her in the future.
Your planned tour South of Chile and Argentina is perfectly wonderful. The only place I have not been yet is Lago Argentina. I have heard it is beautiful and very interesting. The lake is cut in two by a glacier until the pressure of one side is so great that the ice dam breaks producing thundering sounds which you can see from very close. It seems this happens once a year, and if you hit it in time it is a wonderful experience.
Unless your paid tour includes your stay in Santiago at the Sheraton Hotel, what can be more comfortable to you, you are welcome to stay with me and so spend less. Anyhow you could start having breakfast at my place if you choose to stay in the Hotel.
*the day
