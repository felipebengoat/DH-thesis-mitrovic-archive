HOTEL CONTINENTAL
CAMILO ALZUGET
CASILLA 72 - TELÉFONO 166
TEMUCO
DIRECCIÓN TELEGRÁFICA "CONTINENTAL"
HAY AUTO DEL HOTEL A TODOS LOS TRENES
Temuco, Oct. 7 de 1937
Dear Lucho!
We are back again from Villarrica and are proceeding South to Osorno this afternoon from where we shall make a little "Abstecher" into the country. The money from Judson was here, so I am at last in funds, which is a great relief I can grant you.
After Osorno we will probably continue a bit further South to Puerto Montt for a couple of days. I think that I might just as well take advantage of the chance I have got of seeing the country down here now as I, in all probability, will not have it later.
Jacques Choung has returned to Santiago so you may be seeing him and hear about our trip so far. Daniel De Wolff, López and I are the only ones left now.
I would very much like to have my mail because I have had no news from home since leaving Temuco. Would you take the letters that have arrived for me and put them in one big envelope and send it down to Osorno - to the main post office - "Poste restante" or however you call it (in Europe Poste Restante). And send the letter registered.
Also please enclose a note to tell me how the films were developed in Santiago and any other news. Also if a package has come for me from Europe (the one with my photo enlargements and books). Also how I stand in getting back the $30.- from Argentine Consulate.
Regarding the letter you had better address it to DANIEL DE WULF as I have no pass or other means of identification and they might therefore hesitate to give me a registered letter. Please send it EXPRESSO.
Best wishes to your father and mother and thanks to you for the trouble.
Tjark
