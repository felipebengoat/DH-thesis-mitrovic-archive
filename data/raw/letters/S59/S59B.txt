Your letter of 8.8.1935 addressed to the Secretariat of the Technical University Berlin was forwarded to us for reply. We take the liberty of informing you of the following:
Naturally, the young South American mentioned by you has the possibility of continuing his architectural studies here, particularly from a technical standpoint. The study of architecture comprises 8 semesters, in addition to six months of practical work on a construction site, which must be completed before beginning the course of study. After 4 academic semesters, the student in question may sit his preliminary diploma examination, and after a further 4 semesters his main diploma examination. He will then receive the title of Diplom-Ingenieur and will be a fully trained architect.
For your further information regarding the formalities to be completed for the admission of foreign students, we are sending you by the same post a course catalogue of our Technical University, from which you can find all relevant details on page 11. Furthermore, this catalogue will also allow you to familiarize yourself precisely with the subjects offered in the lectures here.
