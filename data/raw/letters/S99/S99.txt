Isola d'Ischia, July 3, 99
Dear Lucho, Lorna and I are nearing the end of 10 marvelous days in Ravello, Positano, Capri (full of Americans) and now Ischia (full of Germans & Italians — no Americans) and Naples which all avoid (fear of crime) but a very rich city. The Bourbon Palace at Caserta rivals Versailles, a galleria modeled on Milan and a magnificent opera house etc etc. Pompeii has been further excavated since my first visit in '55. We hope you are well. We read that So. American economies including Chile still struggling. I must call you soon. Our love — Howard
