Dear Luis:
We have completed our plans for skiing near Mallnitz. Hope that you (and your friend) find it possible to come along as I am sure it will be fine skiing as well as a lot of fun.
I leave Vienna, Sunday, March 30, 21:35 o'clock, arriving in Salzburg 6:42 Monday morning, where I am going to stay for a day and night in order to have a good look at the town. Tuesday morning 7:00 the train leaves for Mallnitz, arriving 11:02. Brunnar comes from his home in Kärnten, arriving in Mallnitz at 14:28. And we then start climbing up to the Alpenverein Hütte. We stay up skiing Wednesday to Wednesday (April 1-8 inclusive), giving us one full week's skiing.
If you can come, we could meet in Salzburg, Monday. The 7:45 train from Munich gets you into Salzburg at 9:40 and we could then continue on to Mallnitz together as given in the schedule above.
The ski hut we are going to is closed, but we have rented the key from the Alpenverein. Food has to be taken along, and we do our own cooking. So far Brunnar, a friend of his, I, and possibly a girl he knows are going. You and your friend would make 6, just the right sized group. The Hütten lie between 2500 and 3000 meters; the snow, therefore, is bound to be ideal.
Let me know as soon as possible if you can come.
Regards to you and Papa Brown. Winold.
PS: We have your training shirt and will bring it along if you can come or send it to you otherwise.
